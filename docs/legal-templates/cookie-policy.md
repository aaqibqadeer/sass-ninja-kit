## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites function
and remember information about your visit.

## 2. How we use cookies

- **Strictly necessary:** required for the Service to work — e.g. the session
  cookie (`ninjakit_session`) that keeps you signed in, and the active-workspace
  cookie (`ninjakit_active_org`). These cannot be switched off.
- **Preferences:** remember choices such as your cookie-consent selection
  (`ninjakit_cookie_consent`).
- **Analytics (optional):** help us understand usage via [ANALYTICS_PROVIDER].
  Loaded only if you accept.
- **Marketing (optional):** [MARKETING_COOKIE_DETAILS or "not used"].
